Q · 为什么这样建?

Why I build the way I do.

"Most AI products treat low-resource languages and emerging markets as English-first with a translation layer on top. I think that's the wrong abstraction, and the systems I build try to prove it."

My way of thinking about AI products came out of my undergrad thesis at NPU (ADCMOplus · with Prof. Lin Li) on constrained dynamic multi-objective optimization. AI products almost never come down to a single metric — you're trading latency, cost, accuracy, and fairness against each other, and the honest answer is a Pareto front, not a winner. The six-check verifier in jak.ma is the same reasoning applied at runtime.

Practically, this means I work in Arabic, Darija, French, English, and functional Mandarin — I can ship products into MENA, work inside a Chinese tech stack, and connect the two without the usual translation cost.

jak.ma · Moroccan home-services marketplace

Zero-commission, anti-shnaq, Darija-first chat marketplace for skilled-trade workers (plumbers, electricians, tilers). 1,862 workers · 15 cities · 3,400 daily queries · 1,100 DAU. Two-pass architecture: Pass 1 classifies intent + city, Pass 2 generates a constrained response that must clear a six-check verifier before reaching the user. p50 ≈ 1.2s · ≈$0.0008 per query · <1% verifier rejection in production. The price-band check is built from a 200-worker field survey — there's no published price reference for Moroccan trades, and an LLM-as-judge would just rubber-stamp the model's biased prior. Beats sabab.ma (French-first incumbent) on worker count (4× more).

Q · 我怎么做产品?

How do I think about AI products?

The honest answer is a Pareto front, not a winner. Classify the failure mode first, then pick the right tool.

AI products almost never come down to a single metric — you're trading latency, cost, accuracy, and fairness against each other. That's the through-line from my undergrad thesis (ADCMOplus · constrained dynamic multi-objective optimization) to the runtime architecture of jak.ma.

My signature methodology — 5-dim evaluation rubric + verifier-gated retrieval — started at DOPEDCLUB (Casablanca), got formalised at Baidu ERNIE (Shenzhen), and now runs in production at jak.ma (Morocco) as a six-check verifier.
